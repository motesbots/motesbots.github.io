SMART HOTELS

Your Guest’s mobile is their Room Key

SIMPLE.  FAST.  SECURE.

Welcome to a new era of hotel entry! It’s key-less, card-less, and completely reshaping the standard for guest service.

The hotel check-in process hasn’t changed much over the years. You walk in and wait … When you can handle everything else in your life, from either your phone or laptop, it seems strange that you still have to check-in at a counter.

“If I can book my hotel, skip the line at the airport, and order a taxi all from my phone, why can’t I check into my room?”

For Guests: How it Works

STEP 1

Download the MobiKey App on your Android or iOS device.

STEP 2

Create a MobiKey Account.

STEP 3

Check-in to your Hotel from the App.

STEP 4

Receive a notification when your room is ready.

STEP 5

Use your phone to unlock your room's door.

For Hotels: How it Works

STEP 1

Login to the MobiKey Front Desk Platform.

STEP 2

Review and approve guests requesting check-in.

STEP 3

Issue guests with digital keys.

 

STEP 4

Add up additional guests per room and easily revoke keys instantly.
